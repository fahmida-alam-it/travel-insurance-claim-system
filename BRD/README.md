# Business Requirements Documents (BRD) – Travel Insurance Claim System

This folder contains all **Business Requirement Documents (BRD)** for the Travel Insurance Claim System. These documents define the business needs, objectives, and high-level requirements for the system from a stakeholder perspective.

---

## Purpose of BRD

The BRD serves to:

- Capture **business goals, functional requirements, and constraints**.
- Provide a clear understanding of the **system objectives** to stakeholders, analysts, and developers.
- Serve as a reference throughout the project lifecycle to ensure that the system meets business expectations.
- Support alignment between business needs and technical implementation.

---

## Contents

| File Name | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| `Travel_Insurance_Claim_System_BRD.docx` | Main BRD in Word format, containing all business requirements, objectives, and diagrams. |
| `Travel_Insurance_Claim_System_BRD.md` | Markdown version of the BRD for easier viewing in GitHub. |
| `Diagrams/` | Subfolder containing visual representations supporting the BRD, including ER diagrams, flowcharts, and UML diagrams. |

---

## Notes

- This folder complements the **System Requirements Documents (SRD)**, which provide detailed technical specifications derived from the BRD.
- Keep the BRD updated whenever business requirements change.
- Refer to the diagrams in the `Diagrams/` subfolder to visualize workflows, data models, and entity relationships.
