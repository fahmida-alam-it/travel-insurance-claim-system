# Travel Insurance Claim System
System Requirements Document (SRD)

## 1. System Overview
The Travel Insurance Claim System enables customers to submit travel-related insurance claims and allows claims adjusters to review, approve, or reject submitted claims.

## 2. System Actors
- Customer
- Claims Adjuster
- System Administrator

## 3. Functional Requirements

### FR-001 Claim Submission
The system shall allow customers to submit travel insurance claims through an online claim form.

### FR-002 Document Upload
The system shall allow customers to upload supporting documents such as receipts and medical reports.

### FR-003 Claim Review
The system shall allow claims adjusters to view submitted claims and supporting documentation.

### FR-004 Claim Approval or Rejection
The system shall allow claims adjusters to approve or reject submitted claims.

### FR-005 Claim Status Tracking
The system shall allow customers to track the status of their submitted claims.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

### NFR-001 Security
The system shall ensure secure authentication and authorization.

### NFR-002 Performance
System response time shall not exceed 3 seconds.

### NFR-003 Availability
The system shall be available 99.5% of the time.
